Craftsmanship Perfected Over Generations

For over three decades, Mercy Fashion Design has been crafting exquisite bespoke garments for discerning gentlemen. Our journey began in 1985 when master tailor Alessandro Ricci founded our atelier with a vision to create suits that combine traditional craftsmanship with contemporary style. Every stitch tells a story of dedication, precision, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Our team of master tailors, trained in the finest European traditions, work meticulously to create garments that don't just fit perfectly but reflect the unique personality of each client.
